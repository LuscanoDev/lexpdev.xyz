All the satellites orbiting the Earth and taking some form of image have to send that image back to Earth. To do this, some satellites use radio to transmit the image, and anyone with the right radio can listen in and decode these images. In this album, there will be some images that I managed to receive from these satellites. (Hover over the image for more details)
